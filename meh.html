    TO PREPARE HENNA :

  • Collect dried powdered henna leaves.
  • Sieve the powder to obtain a fine mossy green powder.
  • Combine the powder with water, lemon juice, drops of eucalyptus oil, and mix till a smooth paste is obtained.
  • Apply the paste using a variety of techniques.
  • Leave the paste on the skin for eight hours.
  • After it is removed, the pattern continues to darken for approximately three days.
